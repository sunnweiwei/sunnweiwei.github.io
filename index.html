Weiwei Sun

I am a PhD student at LTI, Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Yiming Yang. Before that, I received my M.E. and B.E. from Shandong University, advised by Zhaochun Ren.
My recent research focuses on LLM agents for long-horizon [FoldAgent/SUPO] scientific reasoning [CoMind/Co-Bench/FrontierCO/CodePDE] and information retrieval [ZeroGR/MMOA-RAG/ReasonRank].
